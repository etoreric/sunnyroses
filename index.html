ABOUT SUNNYROSES

Empowering students to achieve their dreams through quality education

SunnyRoses International School is a co-educational private school committed to quality education. SunnyRoses International School was established in 2006 at the Westlands Boulevard, West Legon and then moved to its current premises near Dome-Kwabenya Road. It can be accessed by residents in Dome, Kwabenya, Ashongman, Pokuase, Madina, Taifa and environs. We believe in developing the child holistically so apart from academic curricular, we also have extra curricular activities such as Tae-kwando, Basketball, Football, Reading Club, Cadet, Coding and Robotics, Drama, French etc. Our facilities include an ICT Centre, Library, a Montessori learning Centre, multifunctional playground.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that fosters intellectual growth, creativity, and character development.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that inspires students to excel and make a positive impact on the world.

Our Academic Programs

At SunnyRoses International School, we are committed to providing a world-class education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning. Our academic program is designed to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to excel in an ever-changing global landscape. With a focus on holistic development, we strive to create an environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
