About Our School

SunnyRoses International School is a co-educational private school committed to quality education. SunnyRoses International School was established in 2006 at the Westlands Boulevard, West Legon and then moved to its current premises near Dome-Kwabenya Road. It can be accessed by residents in Dome, Kwabenya, Ashongman, Pokuase, Madina, Taifa and environs. We believe in developing the child holistically so apart from academic curricular, we also have extra curricular activities such as Tae-kwando, Swimming, Basketball, Football, Reading Club, Cadet, Coding and Robotics, Drama, French etc. Our facilities include an ICT Centre, Library, a Montessori learning Centre, multifunctional playground and a science lab.

Our goal is to build an environment that will help the child to explore, communicate, work and perfect skills to his/her highest potential. We envisage working with parents to help develop the child holistically, which encompasses developing the child's concentration, independence, confidence, order and self-discipline

Academics

At Sunny Roses International School, we are committed to academic excellence and fostering a love for learning. Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students while equipping them with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in an ever-changing world.

We take pride in preparing students for higher education and future careers. Our guidance counselors provide academic advising, college preparation resources, and career exploration opportunities to ensure that students are ready for the next step in their educational journey.
At Sunny Roses International School, we believe that learning goes beyond the classroom. We encourage critical thinking, collaboration, and a lifelong love of discovery. Whether your child is just beginning their educational journey or preparing for college, our academic programs will provide the foundation for success.
