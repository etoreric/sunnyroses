Our Academic Programs

At SunnyRoses International School, we are committed to providing a world-class education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning. Our academic program is designed to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to excel in an ever-changing global landscape. With a focus on holistic development, we strive to create an environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Crèche
6 months - 2 years

Our nurturing crèche provides a safe, stimulating environment for our youngest learners with sensory play, early socialization, and developmental activities.

  • Individual care plans
  • Early stimulation
  • Safe, child-proof environment
Kindergarten
3 - 5 years

Our play-based kindergarten program fosters curiosity and builds foundational skills through thematic learning, creative arts, and early literacy.

  • Phonics & early math
  • Creative expression
  • Social-emotional development
Lower Primary
6 - 8 years

Building strong academic foundations with engaging, hands-on learning experiences that develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

  • Core subject mastery
  • Project-based learning
  • Character education
Upper Primary
9 - 11 years

Challenging academic program that prepares students for secondary education while encouraging independent learning and leadership.

  • Advanced curriculum
  • STEM integration
  • Leadership opportunities
Junior High
12 - 14 years

Comprehensive program that combines rigorous academics with extracurricular activities to develop well-rounded young adults.

  • Subject specialization
  • College preparation
  • Career exploration
