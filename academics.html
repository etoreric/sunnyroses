Our Academic Programs

At SunnyRoses International School, we are committed to providing a world-class education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love for learning. Our academic program is designed to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and values they need to excel in an ever-changing global landscape. With a focus on holistic development, we strive to create an environment where every student can thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Crèche/ Toddlers

A nurturing environment for our youngest learners (1 yea and above) with play-based early development programs.

  • Individual care plans
  • Early stimulation
  • Safe, child-proof environment

Nursery/Kindergarten

Foundational learning for ages 2 years and above through interactive play, social development, and early literacy.

  • Phonics & early math
  • Creative expression
  • Social-emotional development

Lower & Upper Primary

Grades 1-6 curriculum focusing on core subjects, creativity, and character development.

  • Core subject mastery
  • Project-based learning
  • Character education

Junior High

Grades 7-9 program preparing students for senior high with advanced academics and extracurriculars.

  • High School preparation
  • Career exploration
