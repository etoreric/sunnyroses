ABOUT SUNNYROSES

Empowering students to achieve their dreams through quality education

SunnyRoses International School is a co-educational private school committed to quality education. SunnyRoses International School was established in 2006 at the Westlands Boulevard, West Legon and then moved to its current premises near Dome-Kwabenya Road. It can be accessed by residents in Dome, Kwabenya, Ashongman, Pokuase, Madina, Taifa and environs. We believe in developing the child holistically so apart from academic curricular, we also have extra curricular activities such as Tae-kwando, Swimming, Basketball, Football, Reading Club, Cadet, Coding and Robotics, Drama, French etc. Our facilities include an ICT Centre, Library, a Montessori learning Centre, multifunctional playground.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that fosters intellectual growth, creativity, and character development.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that inspires students to excel and make a positive impact on the world.

About SunnyRoses

Our goal is to build an environment that will help the child to explore, communicate, work and perfect skills to his/her highest potential.
